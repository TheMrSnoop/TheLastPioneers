THE LAST PIONEERS

a space RPG | currently in development

TACTICAL FPS

Gameplay consists of a Singleplayer FPS with tactical elements. The player goes around various missions around places like Earth, The Moon, Mars, Venus, Europa, and Titan. In these missions, the player fights the Soviet Space Forces. Soviet enemies range from Armed Cosmonauts, to Robot Spider-bots and Self-Aware drones.

HISTORICAL FICTION

You are a Soldier in the US Space Force during the height of a new cold war. Fight a modern version of the USSR, and fight to colonize the rest of the solar system.
