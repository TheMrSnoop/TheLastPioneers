Procedurally Generated Animations

Unreal Engine 5.6 allows for the procedural generation of locomotion animations, in real time. I tried implementing this new feature to the Spider bot that I have had lying around for over 5 months now. It turns out, its only really meant for animations, and not games. Either way, it pushed me to finally start developing my own custom version of this feature.


I won't get super technical but basically, each leg shoots invisible rays in order to detect where the ground is, in relation to that leg. Then, the leg will dynamically move in a way so that the foot of the leg, is flat with whatever that line came in contact with. I then have the legs independently move in a way to where it looks like the bot as a whole, is walking.
